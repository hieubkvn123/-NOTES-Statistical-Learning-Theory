# Statement
---
- **Theorem (<u style='color:red'>Zorn's Lemma</u>)**:  
---
<u>Pre-requisites</u>:
- If $P$ is a partially-ordered set.
- Every _chain_ in $P$ has an upper-bound.

<u>Then</u>: 
- $P$ _contains at least one maximal element_.

# Proof
---
- _We will treat this as an axiom_.
